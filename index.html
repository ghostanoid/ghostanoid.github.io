What We Do

Lovitt Games is a bold new voice in the games market. Whether you prefer pen and paper or something more visual, Lovitt Games has something for you!

We start where most people leave for last: the developers. All too often do we hear about crunch time, executive meddling, and so much more controversy surrounding the developers of games we love. Being creative types ourselves, we understand the need for breathing room to allow creativity to flourish. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to a few key tenets to make the best products we can.

Equal Work, Equal Pay

We believe we can operate better without bloated executive paychecks, so we've established a pay scaling method. The highest paid partner cannot make more than twice what we pay the lowest paid member.

 

Livable Wages

By that same merit, we are constantly readjusting our pay schedule to suit our partners' needs. Nobody works well when they're worried about being able to pay their rent, and that goes double for creative jobs. We take our parnter's well-being as a mark of personal pride.

No Shareholders

What good comes from bending over backwards to appease shareholers? None whatsoever! Our product-over-profit methodology means we will always make the games the people want; not what a shareholder's meeting decides!

 

No Crunch

Shigeru Miyamoto once said "A delayed game is eventually good; a bad game is bad forever." We take pride in our games and swear to only deliver the finest we can muster. Delays happen, but we will not put our partners through crunch-time just to meet an arbitrary deadline.

We put our all into everything we do. Whether it's game design, helping each other out, or even participating in local events, Lovitt Games doesn't just put our cards on the table. We put the whole deck on that bad boy. There are no half measures here!

We work hard and we play hard. Ok, we mostly play hard. But that's half the fun, really! When you make top quality tabletop and video games like we do, there's rarely a dull moment!

As a co-op, we're always ensuring our partners have proper work/life balance and that everyone is treated fairly. That's why we're a co-op in the first place! We want everyone's contributions to be recognized, and we want everyone to feel comfortable and at home as we move forward.
